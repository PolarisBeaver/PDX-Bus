Rail Map Hotspots

This HTML files is used to design the hotspots on the TriMet rail map. Use a tool to get the hotspots in the correct place, then copy the map section into the method "initHotspotData" in RailMapView.m, replacing the HTML with macros as explained below.

  • Replace <area shape="poly" coords=" with HOTSPOT_POLYGON
  • Replace " href= with HOTSPOT_ACTION
  • Replace /> with HOTSPOT_END
